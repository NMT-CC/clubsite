NMT Cybersecurity Club

Weekly Workshops · Fridays · 6:30pm · Bring A Computer!

We are a collection of hackers who welcome all, experienced or not, to join us for weekly, hands-on workshops that explore everything from malware reversing, to cryptography, to lock-picking. Additionally, we compete monthly in online, international hacking competitions as a team.

Competitions

HackTM CTF Quals 2023

[CTF TIME]
WreckTheLine and HackTM have teamed up once again and we're very glad to announce the second edition of HackTM CTF! The qualifications round will take place on February 18-19 2023, online, in jeopardy format, and will feature tasks in a variety of categories. The best 10 teams will then qualify to the on-site finals which will take place in the wonderful city of Timisoara, Romania on May 2023. Each team will send up to 4 members, which will receive partial compensation for the travel expenses. Stay tuned for chall teasers and prize announcements on our twitter: https://twitter.com/WreckTheLine Infra sponsored by goo.gle/ctfsponsorship.
Sat, 18 Feb. 2023, 12:00 UTC — Sun, 19 Feb. 2023, 12:00 UTC

Hack-A-Sat 4 Qualifiers

[CTF TIME]
This prize competition is meant to create a two-way bridge between traditional cyber security professionals who are accustomed to categories like web, forensics, crypto, binaries, protocols, etc, and satellite designers and operators who think in terms of TLEs, quaternions, and two body problems. Moonlighter is the world’s first purpose-built satellite just for cybersecurity training and research. It’s literally a hacking sandbox in space, that will be launched in 2023 and host the HAS4 Finals competition.
Sat, 01 April 2023, 00:00 UTC — Mon, 03 April 2023, 00:00 UTC
